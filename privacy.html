Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Austin AI Automation Agency ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, and interact with our platform.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services. By accessing and using our platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, job title, and other contact details when you create an account or sign up for our services.
  • Communication Data: Messages, emails, feedback, and inquiries you send us through contact forms, support channels, or direct communication.
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details, and transaction history when you purchase our services (processed securely by third-party payment providers).
  • Workflow Data: Information about your business processes, automation workflows, and data that you input into our platform for automation purposes.

2. Information Collected Automatically

When you interact with our platform, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Log Data: Server logs containing IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and timestamps of your activities.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access our services, including device type, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including features used, workflows created, and time spent on various sections.
  • Location Information: General location data derived from IP address (city, region, country level) to provide localized services.

3. Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party sources:

  • Integrated Applications: When you connect third-party applications to our platform, we receive data as authorized by you and those applications' terms.
  • Business Partners: Information shared by partners or affiliates with your consent or as part of joint services.
  • Publicly Available Sources: Information available from public databases or business directories for verification purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes:

1. Service Delivery and Operations

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our automation platform and services
  • Processing transactions and sending related information
  • Creating and managing your account and user profile
  • Executing your automation workflows and managing integrations

2. Communication and Support

  • Responding to your inquiries and providing customer support
  • Sending service announcements, updates, and administrative messages
  • Providing training materials and documentation
  • Sending promotional materials and newsletters (with your consent)

3. Analytics and Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve platform functionality and user experience
  • Conducting research and developing new features
  • Monitoring platform performance and security
  • Creating aggregate, anonymized analytics and reports

4. Security and Compliance

  • Preventing fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Enforcing our terms of service and other agreements
  • Complying with legal obligations and law enforcement requests
  • Maintaining audit logs and security records

5. Marketing and Business Development

  • Sending marketing communications about new features and services
  • Conducting surveys and gathering feedback
  • Creating case studies and success stories (with permission)

Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website or use our platform. They help us recognize you, remember your preferences, and improve your user experience. Cookies can be session-based (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent (stored for a longer period).

Types of Cookies We Use

  • 1

    Essential Cookies

    Required for basic platform functionality, security, and authentication. These cookies are necessary for the platform to operate properly and cannot be disabled.

  • 2

    Preference Cookies

    Store your preferences and settings such as language, theme, and interface customizations to enhance your user experience.

  • 3

    Analytics Cookies

    Help us understand how users interact with our platform, including which features are most popular and how users navigate the interface.

  • 4

    Marketing Cookies

    Used to track your interactions for marketing purposes, personalize advertisements, and measure campaign effectiveness (used only with your consent).

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, blocking essential cookies may impair platform functionality. You can also opt-out of certain types of cookies through our cookie preference center.

Please note that third-party services integrated with our platform (such as analytics providers) may also use cookies. Refer to their privacy policies for more information about their cookie practices.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security practices include:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols (256-bit encryption).
  • Access Controls: Role-based access controls and multi-factor authentication limit access to sensitive data.
  • Data Isolation: Customer data is logically and physically isolated to prevent cross-account access.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits, penetration testing, and vulnerability assessments.
  • Backup and Recovery: Regular automated backups ensure data can be recovered in case of incidents.

While we implement strong security measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. The retention period varies depending on the type of information:

  • Account Information: Retained for the duration of your account and for a reasonable period afterward for legal and business purposes.
  • Workflow Data: Retained as long as your account is active, or as specified in your service agreement.
  • Log Data: Typically retained for 12 months, except where longer retention is required by law.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for business analysis.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of your personal information that we hold.
  • Right to Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations).
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt-out of marketing communications and certain data processing activities.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured, commonly used format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below. We will respond to your request within the timeframe required by applicable law.

Third-Party Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share information with vendors and service providers who assist us in operating our platform and conducting our business (subject to confidentiality agreements).
  • Business Partners: We may share aggregated, anonymized information with partners for joint marketing or research purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfers: If we merge, are acquired, or sell assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • With Your Consent: We share information when you explicitly consent to such sharing for specific purposes.

International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you are accessing our services from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules.

We implement appropriate safeguards for international data transfers, including standard contractual clauses and adequacy decisions where applicable.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
